Drone Flight Training

Students learn FAA safety rules, responsible flying, and hands-on flight skills using our training drones. From pre-flight checks to controlled maneuvers — real aviation concepts, real skills.

  • FAA Safety Rules
  • Hands-On Flight
  • Pre-flight Checks

STEM Workshops

Interactive classroom workshops connecting drone technology to core STEM concepts — physics of flight, basic programming, data collection, and real-world aerospace applications.

  • Physics of Flight
  • Basic Coding
  • Data Collection

Team Challenges

Students compete in timed precision-flying challenges, mission simulations, and obstacle courses — building communication, strategy, and sportsmanship alongside technical skills.

  • Obstacle Courses
  • Teamwork
  • Mission Sims

Engineering Activities

Hands-on design and build challenges where students prototype drone attachments, map flight routes, analyze aerodynamics, and present their engineering solutions to peers.

  • Prototyping
  • Aerodynamics
  • Design Thinking
